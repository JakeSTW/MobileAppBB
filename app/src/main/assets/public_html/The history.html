The History Of The NBA

The NBA was first founded 6th June 1946, but basketball itself was invented 1st December 1891

The 90's

There were many players that changed the game of basketball and some of them even made rule changes, such as players like wilt chamberlin, were able to block shots before they went in, so they had to add in the goaltending rule.

The 2000's

The 2000's had many great players such as legendary Kobe Bryant and Lebron James who made the game of basketball even more popular, it also had icons such as Allen Iverson who popularised dribbling and a clothing style.

The 2010's

The 2010's have made the game of basketball much different then it was when it first was around, players have made the game more technical and more skillful, such as James Harden popularising the step back move and Steph Curry making the 3pt line even more popular.
